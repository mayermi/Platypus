How it works

How can I contribute?

  • Share an idea for the city for all to elaborate
  • Share modification suggestions for ideas to elaborate them
  • Add additions to modifications that are still missing somehing or are too vague
  • Like/dislike modifications and additions to influence which will be included in the idea

When/How get modifications and additions integrated in the idea?

After each phase modifications and their additions people voted for get merged by the modification author.

Each idea elaboration has three phases:

  • Initialization (share your initial thoughts)
  • Reflection (share your thoughts after having seen the others' points)
  • Reinvestigation (share your thoughts after especially investigating unpopular points)

Which modifications & additions are added?

Initiation & Reflection Phase:

Modifications that have only likes and definete additions (only likes or dislikes -the additions with only likes will be integrated as well). This way people can still elaborate modifications they are devided over.

Reinvestigation Phase:

Here we use the majority vote system so that elaborated discussions cane have a result.

Whats up with all that reasoning?

Platypus wants you to work and elaborate ideas together. This can get difficult when people with different opinions, backgrounds and knoledge etc. come together.

So reasoning, explaining why you suggest something, is important for everyone to understand and learn from each other.

What kind of reasoning helps: Grounded facts (include references if possible), anecdotes, experiences, etc.

What does not help: Anything only stating a subjective opinion or similar.

Rules & Etiquette

We come together to create new things. To take the first step and find city developments for the citizen. Play fair.

Selfish behavior will not help our elaborations for finding solutions. Neither do discrimination, rudeness, or anything that aims at ?.

We are a community, and we respect each other even if we have different opinions. This is a place to share and learn, and to ultimately learn to understand each other.

Stay nice, do not take opposing opinons personal and help create our future city together!
